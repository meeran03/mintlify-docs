# Welcome to Burki Voice AI

**Burki** is a comprehensive, AI-powered voice assistant platform designed to handle customer calls with advanced features like real-time audio processing, multi-tenant support, and seamless integrations with leading AI and telephony providers.

## Who is Burki for?
- Startups and enterprises looking to automate and enhance customer communication
- Developers building voice-driven applications
- Organizations needing multi-assistant, multi-organization call management

## What can Burki do?
- **Handle phone calls** using Twilio and real-time WebSocket streaming
- **Transcribe speech** instantly with Deepgram
- **Generate intelligent responses** using OpenAI, ElevenLabs, and other LLMs
- **Convert text to natural speech** with multiple TTS providers
- **Denoise and enhance audio** for crystal-clear conversations
- **Manage multiple assistants and phone numbers** from a modern web dashboard
- **Secure, multi-tenant architecture** with robust authentication and role-based access

## Why use Burki?
- **Production-ready**: Built for reliability, scalability, and security
- **Flexible**: Integrates with your favorite AI and telephony tools
- **Beautiful dashboard**: Manage, monitor, and analyze calls and assistants in real time
- **Open source**: Fully extensible and customizable for your needs
