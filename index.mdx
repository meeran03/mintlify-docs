# Welcome to Burki Voice AI

**The Voice AI Platform Built for Performance**

**Burki** is the complete voice AI platform delivering what others promise but fail to provide. With ultra-low latency (0.8-1.2s vs 4-5s industry average), simple pricing, and a beautiful interface that actually works, Burki is the smart choice for businesses building voice-powered applications.

## Who is Burki for?
- Startups and enterprises looking to automate and enhance customer communication
- Developers building voice-driven applications
- Organizations needing multi-assistant, multi-organization call management

## What can Burki do?
- **Handle phone calls** using Twilio, Vonage, or Telnyx with real-time WebSocket streaming
- **Transcribe speech** instantly with Deepgram
- **Generate intelligent responses** using OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, and more
- **Convert text to natural speech** with multiple TTS providers
- **Denoise and enhance audio** for crystal-clear conversations
- **Manage multiple assistants and phone numbers** from a modern web dashboard
- **Secure, multi-tenant architecture** with robust authentication and role-based access

## Why choose Burki?

### Ultra-Low Latency
- **0.8-1.2 seconds** response time for natural conversations
- **Built-in Audio Denoising** with RNNoise for crystal-clear calls
- **Production-Ready** architecture that scales with your business

### Simple & Flexible Pricing
- **Pay-as-you-go** with no monthly minimums
- **Burki Cloud** for fully managed simplicity
- **BYO Mode** to use your own API keys and control costs
- **Hybrid** to mix and match as needed

### Beautiful & Usable
- **Modern Dashboard** that makes configuration intuitive
- **All-in-One Platform** with everything you need built-in
- **Comprehensive API** for full programmatic control
