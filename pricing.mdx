# Simple, Transparent Pricing

Burki offers straightforward pricing with no hidden fees. Pay only for what you use, with generous free trial credits to get started.

---

## Platform Fees

Every customer pays a simple platform fee based on usage:

| Service | Platform Fee |
|---------|-------------|
| **Voice Calls** | $0.03 per connected minute |
| **SMS Messages** | $0.001 per segment |

<Info>
**Connected minutes** are billed only when a call is actually answered. If a call isn't connected, you're not charged.
</Info>

## Burki Cloud Pricing

When using Burki Cloud, we manage all AI providers for you:

| Component | How It's Billed |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Platform Fee | $0.03/min (voice), $0.001/segment (SMS) |
| Carrier Costs | Pass-through at cost |
| LLM (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) | Pass-through at cost |
| STT (Deepgram, Azure) | Pass-through at cost |
| TTS (Cartesia, ElevenLabs) | Pass-through at cost |
| Managed Service Fee | +15% on all pass-through costs |
| Phone Numbers | $1.00/month (prorated daily) |

<Accordion title="Example: 5-minute Burki Cloud call">
| Fee Type | Calculation | Amount |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Platform Fee | 5 min × $0.03 | $0.15 |
| Carrier Pass-through | Vonage cost | $0.10 |
| LLM Pass-through | OpenAI cost | $0.10 |
| STT Pass-through | Deepgram cost | $0.25 |
| TTS Pass-through | Cartesia cost | $0.50 |
| Managed Fee (15%) | ($0.10 + $0.10 + $0.25 + $0.50) × 15% | $0.14 |
| **Total** | | **$1.24** |
</Accordion>

## BYO (Bring Your Own) Pricing

When you bring your own API keys, you only pay platform fees:

| Component | How It's Billed |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Platform Fee | $0.03/min (voice), $0.001/segment (SMS) |
| All AI Providers | You pay directly (not through Burki) |

<Accordion title="Example: 5-minute BYO call">
| Fee Type | Calculation | Amount |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Platform Fee | 5 min × $0.03 | $0.15 |
| LLM/STT/TTS/Carrier | Paid directly to providers | $0.00 |
| **Total to Burki** | | **$0.15** |
</Accordion>

## Hybrid Mode

Mix and match! Use your own keys for some services while letting Burki manage others.

<Accordion title="Example: BYO LLM + Managed STT/TTS (5-minute call)">

|----------|-------------|--------|

| **Total to Burki** | | **$1.02** |

<Info>
The 15% managed fee only applies to services that Burki manages. BYO services have no managed fee.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is voice call duration calculated?">
    We bill based on **connected minutes** only. Time is calculated from when the call is answered until it ends, rounded up to the nearest minute. Unanswered calls are not billed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What counts as an SMS segment?">
    A standard SMS segment is 160 characters. Messages longer than 160 characters are split into multiple segments. We use the carrier's reported segment count.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I switch between BYO and Burki Cloud?">
    Yes! You can change your configuration at any time from the dashboard. You can even use hybrid mode with some services on BYO and others on Burki Cloud.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when my trial ends?">
    Your account continues to work - you'll just start being charged for usage. Add funds to your wallet before the trial ends to ensure uninterrupted service.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a monthly minimum or commitment?">
    No. Burki is pure pay-as-you-go. No monthly minimums, no long-term contracts. Pay only for what you use.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
